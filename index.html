About Us

Our Mission

We devote our time and energy towards bringing hands-on practical experiences to engineering students at RGIPT, as well as create opportunities for students to interact with industry professionals. IEEE student chapter RGIPT is a young body of enthusiastic workforce which strives to learn and contribute towards technological innovations.

Our Vision

The IEEE RGIPT Student Branch envisions a community of innovative engineers and technologists who contribute to society through excellence in technological advancements, fostering a culture of continuous learning, and promoting ethical standards in professional practice. We aim to be a driving force in bridging the gap between academia and industry, providing our members with opportunities for growth, leadership, and collaboration in a global context.
